App Title: Flatmates Bill

App Description: (inputs and outputs) An app that gets as input the amount of a bill
 for a particular period and the days that each of the flatmates stayed in the 
 house for that period and returns how much each flatmate has to play. It also 
 generates a PDF report stating the names of the flatnmates, the period, and how
 much each of them had to pay.
